Our Sustainable Heritage

At Oreplates, we transform the bounty of nature into elegant dinnerware. Our areca leaf plates are crafted with respect for the planet and a commitment to premium quality.

The Spirit of Oreplates

Founded in the fertile palm-rich regions of Tamil Nadu, Oreplates was born from a desire to return to our roots. We saw the harmony in traditional practices and combined them with modern manufacturing to create a product that leaves no trace but leaves a lasting impression.

Our mission is simple: To provide a luxurious dining alternative to plastic that doesn't just "biodegrade" over decades, but nourishes the earth in months. We are committed to empowering local farmers and promoting a truly circular economy.

100% Natural Bounty

Crafted by Nature, Perfected by Us

A simple, chemical-free process that honors the original leaf.

1. Gathering

We only use naturally fallen sheaths of the Areca palm tree, ensuring no trees are ever harmed.

2. Pure Washing

The leaves are cleaned with high-pressure water to remove dust and organic residues.

3. Heat Molding

Leaves are heat-pressed into elegant shapes. No chemicals, adhesives, or dyes are ever used.

4. Quality Check

Each plate is UV sterilized and inspected to ensure it meets our premium durability standards.
