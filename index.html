Our Manufacturing Process

How we turn fallen leaves into premium tableware.

Step 01

Collection & Sorting

We ethically source naturally fallen sheaths from Areca palm trees. Only the best quality sheaths are selected for processing.

Step 02

Cleaning & Heat Pressing

Leaves are thoroughly cleaned and then heat-pressed into various shapes using steam. No chemicals or binders are added during this process.
